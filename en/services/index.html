Frequently Asked Questions

What's the difference between a mini and a full dashboard?

A mini-dashboard focuses on the top 5 most important Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) to give you a quick overview. The full dashboard dives deeper, connects multiple data sources, and includes various analytical pages (e.g., customer analysis, product analysis, etc.).

Can I upgrade my package later?

Absolutely! You can start with the Starter Package, and if you find you need ongoing analysis and greater support, you can upgrade to the Growth Package at any time.

Is my data confidential?

Yes, the confidentiality of your data is my utmost priority. I am always prepared to sign a Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA) before starting any project to ensure your data is fully protected.
