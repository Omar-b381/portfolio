Frequently Asked Questions

What kind of data can you analyze?

I can work with various types of data, including sales data, customer data (CRM), website user behavior, financial data, and more. As long as the data is available digitally, it can be analyzed.

How long does a project take?

The timeline depends on the project's scope and complexity. A small dashboard project might take a few days, while an in-depth strategic analysis could take a week or more. I will provide a clear timeline after discussing your project details.

Is my data confidential?

Absolutely. Your data's confidentiality is my top priority. I am fully prepared to sign a Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA) before starting any project to ensure your data is completely protected.
