Analysis of Commercial Records Performance Indicators in Saudi Arabia

A case study on analyzing commercial records data to assess business growth, geographical distribution, and sectoral diversity of investment in the Kingdom.

Power BIPerformance AnalysisKPIsGeographic Analysis

Task

Build an analytical dashboard to measure and track the growth of new businesses and their geographical and sectoral distribution.

Tools Used

Power BI, DAX

Key Result

Identified that the "Wholesale and Retail Trade" sector is the most active and that the "Riyadh" region accounts for the largest share of new records.

üéØ Situation

Government agencies and decision-makers constantly need a precise understanding of the business environment in the Kingdom. Commercial records data is a vital indicator of economic health, but it is often large and complex, making it difficult to extract quick insights.

üìù Task

The task was to design and develop an interactive Power BI dashboard to analyze commercial records data for 2018, aiming to track Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) such as the number of new records, their distribution by regions and cities, and the most active economic sectors.

üõ†Ô∏è Action

To achieve this goal, I took the following steps:

  1. Data Modeling: Built a data model linking commercial records, regions, cities, and economic sectors.
  2. Geographic Analysis: Used interactive Power BI maps to display the distribution of commercial records across the Kingdom, making it easy to identify major economic centers.
  3. Sectoral Analysis: Created charts showing the distribution of new businesses across various sectors (such as construction, manufacturing, wholesale trade) and identifying promising sectors.
  4. Temporal Analysis: Added time filters allowing the user to analyze records growth on a monthly or quarterly basis.

üìà Result

The dashboard became a powerful tool for decision support, providing:

  • A Comprehensive and Quick Overview: Enabling users to get a complete picture of the business environment in seconds instead of reading long reports.
  • Identifying Investment Opportunities: Helping investors identify regions and sectors experiencing increasing growth.
  • Supporting Government Policies: Providing accurate data for decision-makers to evaluate the impact of economic policies and direct support to the sectors and regions that need it.
