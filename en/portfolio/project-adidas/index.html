Comprehensive Analysis of Adidas Sales

A case study on analyzing sales data to identify the most profitable products, most effective channels, and geographic customer trends.

Power BISales AnalysisPricing StrategyEDA

Task

Understand sales performance across different product lines and regions to identify growth opportunities and improve marketing strategies.

Tools Used

Power BI, DAX, Excel

Key Result

Identified that "Men's Street Footwear" generates the highest profit margin and that "Online" sales are the fastest-growing channel.

üéØ Situation

As one of the world's largest sports brands, Adidas has a vast amount of sales data from multiple sources (physical stores, outlets, online sales). The company needed to consolidate and analyze this data to get a comprehensive view of its product performance and determine optimal strategies to increase profits.

üìù Task

My task was to analyze a detailed Adidas sales dataset to answer key business questions such as: What are the best-selling products? Which sales methods generate the highest revenue? How does sales performance differ between regions?

üõ†Ô∏è Action

To achieve this, I built an interactive Power BI dashboard that went through the following stages:

  1. Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA): Examined the initial data to understand its structure and identify Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) to track, such as total sales, total profit, and units sold.
  2. Dashboard Design: Created a clear user interface that displays key metrics at the top, with detailed charts for analyzing performance by product, region, sales method, and time period.
  3. Profitability Analysis: Added analyses focusing on the "Operating Profit Margin" for each product, providing deeper insight than just looking at total sales.

üìà Result

The dashboard provided strategic insights that enabled Adidas to:

  • Improve Inventory Management: By identifying the most profitable products, the company could optimize purchasing and inventory decisions to focus on higher-return items.
  • Direct Marketing Strategies: The analysis revealed that online sales were growing significantly, prompting the company to increase its investment in digital marketing channels.
  • Customize Regional Offers: Understanding the differences in customer preferences between regions allowed for the design of tailored marketing campaigns and product offerings for each area.
